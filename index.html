Puyi

The last emperor of China

Here's a time line of Puyi's life:

  • 1906 - Born in Beijing, China
  • 1908 - Crowned as the emperor of China
  • 1912 - Forced abdication in 1912, after the Xinhai Revolution
  • 1917 - Was restored to the throne from July 1 to July 12
  • 1922 - Married with Wanrong and Wenxiu
  • 1924 - Was expelled from the Forbidden City
  • 1925-1931 - Moved to the Japanese Concession of Tianjin, China
  • 1932-1945 - Puppet ruler of Manchukuo occupied by Japan
  • 1945-1950 - Prisoner in Russia
  • 1950-1959 - Prisoner in China
  • 1959 - Released from prison as a ordinary citizen
  • 1967 - Dies at the age of 61.

Puyi's life is a summary of modern Chinese history.
